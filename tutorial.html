Welcome to Ads Grabber! 🎉

Your tiny, powerful helper for the Meta Ads Library.

🔍 Address Bar Shortcuts (Omnibox)

Jump straight to the Ads Library from your browser's address bar:

Type: @ads + Space + your keywords, then hit Enter.

Examples:
@ads protein powder     → search for "protein powder"
@ads "bald spot"        → exact phrase search
@ads "shop now" -7d     → ads from last 7 days
@ads sale -14d -7d      → ads from 14 to 7 days ago

🖱️ Right-Click & Quick Search

  • From any page: Select any text, right-click, and choose "Search Ads Library for..."
  • Toolbar Button: Click the extension icon on any website to instantly search the Ads Library for that site's domain.

⬇️ Meta Ads Downloader

Inside the Meta Ads Library, you'll see quick download buttons right on ad images and videos. Save creatives directly to your computer!

⚙️ Settings & Customization

Customize how the extension works by right-clicking the extension icon and selecting Options. You can filter results by Media Type and disable/enable features.
